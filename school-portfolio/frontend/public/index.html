History & Mission

Founded in 2004, Sutarpara jatiya vidyalaya has been a beacon of quality education, nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our mission is to provide holistic education that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

Vision

To be a global leader in education, empowering students to become compassionate, innovative, and responsible citizens.

Principal

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it is about building character, fostering creativity, and developing critical thinking. At Elite School, every child discovers their unique potential."
